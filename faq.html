Free Zones offer many benefits, including:
  • 100% foreign ownership.
  • Zero corporate and personal income tax (in most cases).
  • Fast company setup and licensing.
  • Access to global markets with minimal restrictions.

  • Free Zone: Best for international trade, online businesses, or holding companies. Usually not allowed to trade directly within the UAE local market without a distributor.
  • Mainland: Allows you to operate freely across the UAE, but may require a local Emirati sponsor or agent depending on your business activity.
